Haiku about pizza

I enjoy pizza
I really enjoy pizza
I love good pizza

I am fond of cheese
Cheese pizza especially
Cheese pizza tastes great

Pepperoni stuff

my favourite dish

Pineapple pizza
I do not know what to say
about this pizza

Pizza will always
Be my favourite dish
I will never swerve
